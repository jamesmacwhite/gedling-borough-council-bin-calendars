View all bin calendars

Gedling Borough Council operate two different types of bin collections, which are explained below:

Refuse bin collections
These collections include waste and recycling, which is Black Bin (general waste) Green Bin (recycling) and Glass Box (glass recycling).
Garden bin collections
The garden waste bin is an additional paid service and runs differently to the refuse collections.
